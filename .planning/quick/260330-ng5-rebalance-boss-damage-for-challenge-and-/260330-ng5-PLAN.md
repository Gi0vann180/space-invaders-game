<objective>
Two targeted fixes to improve gameplay experience:

1. **Rebalance boss projectile damage** -- Boss attacks currently use `createEnemyProjectile` which deals only 1 damage per hit (same as regular enemies). Boss encounters should feel distinctly more dangerous. Create a dedicated boss projectile factory function with higher damage (2 base, scaling with stage) and slightly larger visual size.

2. **Move HUD above canvas** -- The HUD component is positioned `absolute top-0` inside the same relative container as the canvas, causing it to overlap the top of the gameplay area and cover enemies. Restructure the layout so HUD renders above the canvas in normal document flow.

Purpose: Make the game more challenging during boss fights and eliminate UI/gameplay visual conflict.
Output: Modified projectile, boss attack, HUD, and App files.
</objective>
